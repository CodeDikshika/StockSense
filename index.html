How scoring works
Each enabled parameter contributes a weighted score. Horizon adjusts weights — long-term favours fundamentals (P/E, D/E, future pipeline); short-term favours technicals (MACD, RSI, volume).

Required parameters are hard gates — a stock failing any required param is excluded from the "Passed" count and dimmed.

Optional parameters adjust the composite score only.

Demo data layout:
🧪 Simulated: CMP, day change, P/E, 52W high/low, sector, volume
📐 Computed: RSI (14-day proxy), MACD (12/26/9 EMA signal)
📊 Approximated: Sentiment (momentum proxy), Avg vol% (vs 3M avg)
✏️ Manual (Parameters tab): D/E, face value, future score
